Zula Broken Wing

Hello, my name is ZulaBrokenWing and welcome to my site!

I'm an artist interested in animation and illustration. I also dabble in story telling. My strong suit is character art, especially creature artwork. I plan to expand my art further to specialize in animation, 3D models, and environments.
