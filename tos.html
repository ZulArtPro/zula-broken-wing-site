Below is my Terms of Service (TOS) for commissions.
You must agree to ALL of the conditions set forth below BEFORE applying for a commission.

Section 1: Will/Won't Draw

Below is a general description on what I will and won't draw. Anything not covered in either list or *starred* is subject to discussion on a case by case basis. Please keep my art style in mind while commissioning me, which can be viewed in my gallery and commission examples. As stated in the will NOT section, I won't copy or attempt to mimic another artist's or a company's art style.

I will draw:

  • Feral Animals
  • Anthro Animals
    (ex: dogs, cats, dinosaurs, dragons, mythical creatures, open/closed species, and more!)
  • *Humanoids*
  • *Humans*
  • *Aliens*
  • *Blood/Gore*

I will NOT draw:

  • Not Safe for Work (NSFW) / 18+ rated artwork
  • Fetish Artwork
  • Hateful content in ANY form
  • Replications of another artist or company's art style (ex: Disney, anime, so on)
  • *Robots/Mechs* (Based on complexity)

Section 2: Payment and Refunds

I currently only accept USD as payment through PayPal (subject to gaining more outlets of payment in future).

The commission will not start until payment is received.
It is the commissioner's responsibility to inform payment has been sent before the commission process begins.

Willing to offer payment plans for more expensive commissions. Must be discussed with me before commission(s) have been ordered.

NO REFUNDS once the physical commission process has started/has been completed.
Refunds will only be accepted if the physical commission process hasn't started, which will issue a full refund.
Refunds may also be given if the commission is dropped for whatever reason.

Section 3: Accepting Commissions

  • I can refuse any commission for ANY reason without explanation.
  • I am not obligated to take every commission I receive.
  • I can drop any commission at any time, and will give a full refund in return.
  • I will not accept any commission for “exposure” or for free.
  • My prices are final after an assessment has been made of the commission request.

Section 4: Contact and Discussion

Discussion of the commission can be made through any outlet mentioned in my contact page.

ANY inappropriate conduct by the commissioner (begging for free art, insisting payment in exposure, harassment of the artist or others, breaking of the TOS agreement, and so on) will result in the artist swiftly blocking the commissioner and canceling the commission. (Any payment sent will NOT be refunded).

I, the artist, am not obligated to unblock any commissioner for any reason whatsoever. Once a block has been made, you are BANNED from both contacting and commissioning me.

Section 5: Process

  1. The commissioner must make a request for a commission.
  2. I must approve of the commission before payment is sent. In this phase of the process, a price will be given for your specific commission based upon the request contents.
  3. Payment must be sent before the physical part of the commission process begins. Once payment has been received, the physical commission process will begin.
  4. A sketch of the commission will be made, where it will be communicated between the artist and commissioner for any changes required, before going into the next phase of the process. Here is where the most amount of changes can be requested by the commissioner, afterwards each step following becomes more and more restricted for changes.
  5. Depending on the piece, this part of the process could be very different after the initial sketch. During this phase only a few changes can be made between the artist and the commissioner. Once this step is completed, only finishing touches/tweeks will be added by the artist.
  6. Any final touches will be discussed between the artist and the commissioner. Once this phase is completed, the artwork itself will be final and will not be changed.
  7. The commission is complete, and an unwatermarked version of the commission will be sent to the commissioner.

Section 6: Image Copyright

I, the artist, retain full and exclusive rights to the original artwork. The commissioner can NOT claim the commission artwork as their own artwork or use it for anything other than personal use.

ALL commissioned artwork is for non-commercial use. Redistribution of my work as designs for merchandise, promotion of goods, services, or other forms of monetary gain is strictly prohibited. If there are any further questions, ask me BEFORE commissioning.

Reposting Conditions:

The commissioner is allowed to repost my artwork wherever they like (such as ToyHouse) as long as proper credit is given.
Proper credit includes: The artist's username, and a link back to one of my social media presences or my website.

The artwork created during the commission can be uploaded to my site, or other social media, or uploaded on my personal portfolio, unless stated otherwise by the commissioner.

IF you don't want me to upload the artwork created from the commission process or the final product itself: The commissioner must mention in the commission form/order, that they wish for the artwork created during the physical commission process, to not be distributed by the artist.

Prohibited Actions:

  • Alterations of the artwork without the artist's permission first. (With the exception of cropping/resizing the image for use such as image upload compression and icons for example).
  • Claiming the artwork as your own work and not the artist's.
  • Tracing over/repurposing parts of the artwork.

If these terms are broken, you will be blacklisted from commissioning me and reported.
